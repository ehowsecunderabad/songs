Song 629 - Ay-hamare pitha

Ay-hamare pitha-
Thoo jo aasman me hai
Thera naam paak mana jaaye
Theri baadshaahat aaye

Verse 1
Jaise theri marzi-
Aasmaan me poori hothi
Vaise hi theri marzi-
Jagath me poori hojaye

Verse 2
Rozki roti hamaari '
Aaj hum ko dey dey
Jeevan ki roti thoo hai-
Thoo hi hamara bal hai

Verse 3
Jis thara hum apne-
Aparadhi ko maaf kartey
Usi tarah thoo hamare
Aparadh ko maaf karde

Verse 4
Hum ko azmayishon mein
Jaane thu na dey
Balke burai se bacha
Teri rah main chala

Verse 5
Kyonki rajya aur parakram
Aur teri mahima
Hamesha theri hi hai
Teri rahegi hamesha.
