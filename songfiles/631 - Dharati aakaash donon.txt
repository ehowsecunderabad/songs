Song 631 - Dharati aakaash donon

Dharati aakaash donon,
Prabhu ki aawaaz sunenge,
Sansaar ke sab praani,
Us ka hi naam lenge,
Dharati aakaash donon.

Verse 1
Prabhu ka samay aataa hai,
Dharati magan hogi,
Uski daya barsegi,
Aur door jalan hogi;
Uske vachan ki mahima,
Aur shakti ham dekhenge,
Sansaar ke sab praani,
Us ka hi naam lenge. Dharati

Verse 2
Prabhu ka vachan hai aisa,
Jo chirag sa jalta hai,
Uspar koi chalata hai,
Jeevan use milta hai;
Uske niyam ko jo mane,
Kabhi bhi na woh bhatkega,
Sansaar ke sab praani,
Us ka hi naam lenge Dharati.

Verse 3
Aankhen uthha kar dekho,
Prabhu ka din aayega,
Usko grahan jo karega,
Wohi saath jayega;
Jisne Prabhu ko na maana,
Wohi ek din royega,
Sansaar ke sab praani,
Us ka hi naam lenge. Dharati
