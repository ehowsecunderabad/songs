Song 636 - Aaj ka din Yahova ne

Aaj ka din Yahova ne banaya hai,
Ham isme aanandit hon, aanandit
hon.
Verse 1
Prabhu ko mahima mile,
Chahe ho mera apmaan.
Woh barhe main ghatun,
Rahe isi ka dhyan.
Verse 2
Stuti prashansa karen,
Kyon na kuchh hota rahe.
Usko ham bhaaten rahen,
Chahe jahaan bhi rahen.
Verse 3
Jeevan se jyoti jale,
Jeevan yeh aisa banen.
Jyoti mein chal ham saken,
Yehi dua hai tujhse.
