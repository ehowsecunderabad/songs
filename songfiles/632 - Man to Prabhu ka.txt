Song 632 - Man to Prabhu ka

Man to Prabhu ka mandir hai,
Tan apna daan karo,
Apne Prabhu ki seva mein,
Sab apna daan karo.

Verse 1
Hai yaad tumhe janme the,
Tum paap ke sagar mein,
Aur pane tumhe aayaa,
Woh paap ke sagar mein;
Us pyare Prabhu ke naam ke liye,
Kuchh apna daan karo.

Verse 2
Kal tum jo bhatak gaye the,
Anjaan si raahon mein,
Woh tumko bacha laya,
Apni pyar ki baahon mein;
Jo bhi mila hai Prabhu mein tumhe,
Auron ko daan karo.

Verse 3
Andhkar ke jeevan mein,
Woh roshni le aayaa,
Us roshni mein hamne,
Ek maarg naya paaya;
Jeevan tumhara hai uska,
Jeevan balidaan karo.
