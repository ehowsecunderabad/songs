Song 647 - He jag swami, antaryami

Chorus 1
He jag swami, antaryami,
Tere sanmukh aataa hun

Verse 1
Sanmukh aataa, main sharmaata,
Bhent nahi kuchh laataa hun

Verse 2
Papi jan hun, main nirgun hun,
Dwar tere par ataa hun

Verse 3
Mujh par Yeeshu kripa kijiye,
Paapon se pachhtaata hun

Verse 4
Paap kshamaa kar dijiye mere,
Man se yeh hi chahtaa hun.
