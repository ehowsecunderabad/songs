Song 651 - Dhanyawaad stuti gaayen

Dhanyawaad stuti gaayen,
Hum apne Prabhu ki stuti gaayen
Achcha hai, bhala hai, mera Yeeshu
achchha hai
Mere liye paryaapt hai
Dhanyawaad stuti gaayen,
Hum apne Prabhu ki stuti gaayen

Verse 1
Yariho jaisi badhayen hon,
Aguwayi Yeeshu karega
Darenge nahin, ghabraenge nahin,
Stuti se hum jai payenge

Verse 2
Laal samundar jaisi aazmaishen hon,
Yeeshu tere aage chalega
Vishwas se, aage baro tum,
Saagar mein raaste khulenge

Verse 3
Sharir, praan aur aatma bhi,
Mushkil mein par jaayen to
Stuti geeton ko, gaane se,
Prabhu se saamarth milegi
