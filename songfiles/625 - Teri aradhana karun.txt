Song 625 - Teri aradhana karun

Teri aradhana karun (2)
Pap kshamakar jeevan deydey
Daya ki yachana karun (2)
Theri araadhana karun 'Theri'

Verse 1
Thuhi Mahan, sarva Shakthiman
Thuhi hai mere jeevan ka sangeeth
Hriday ke tar, chedey jhankaar
Theri araadhana hai madhur geeth
Jeevan se mere thu mahima paye
Ek hi kamna karun
Pap kshamakar jeevan deydey
Daya ki yachana karun 'Theri'

Verse 2
Srishti ke har ek kan kan mein,
Chaaya hai teri mahima ka raaj;
Pakshi bhi karte hai teri prashansa,
Har pal sunate hain anand ka rag;
Meri bhi bhakti tujhe grahan ho,
Hriday se prarthana karun;
Pap kshamakar jeevan deydey
Daya ki yachana karun 'Theri'

Verse 3
Patit jeevan me jyoti jala de,
Tujh hi se lagi hain asha meri,
Paapmay tam ko dur hata de,
Purna ho abhilasha meri;
Jeevan ke kathin dukhi kshanon ka,
Dhrirta se samna karun;
Pap kshamakar jeevan deydey
Daya ki yachana karun 'Theri'
