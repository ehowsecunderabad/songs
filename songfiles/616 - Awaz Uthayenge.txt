Song 616 - Awaz Uthayenge

Awaz Uthayenge,
Hum saaz bajayenge
Hai Yishu mahan apna,
Ye geet sunayenge 'Awaz'

Verse 2
Sansar ki sundarta men,
Yeh roop to thera hi,
In chand sitaron mein,
Hai akash to thera hi,
Mahima ki teri baten,
Ham sabko batayenge
Hai Yishu mahan apna,
Yeh geet sunayenge 'Awaz'

Verse 3
Dil tera khazana hai,
Ek pak muhabbat ka
Thah pa na saka koyi,
Saagar hai thu ulphat ka
Ham teri muhabbat se,
Dil apna sajayenge
Hai Yishu mahan apana,
Yeh geet sunayenge 'Awaz'

Verse 4
Na dekh saka hamko,
Thu pap ke saagar mein,
Aur banke manushya aaya,
Aakash se saagar mein
Mukti ka thu data hai,
Ham sabko batayenge,
Hai Yishu mahan apna,
Yeh geet sunayenge 'Awaz'
