Song 634 - Gin, gin ke stuti karun

Gin, gin ke stuti karun,
Beshumar tere danon ke liye,
Ab tak tune sambhala mujhe,
Apni bahon mein liye hue.
Gin, gin ke stuti karun.

Verse 1
Tere shatru ka nishana,
Tujh par hoga na safal,
Aankhon ki putli jaise,
Woh rakhega tujhe har pal.
Gin, gin'..

Verse 2
Aandhiyaan ban ke aayen,
Zindagi ke yeh fikr
Kaun hai tera khewanhara,
Hai bharosa tera kidhar?
Gin, gin'..

Verse 3
Aaye jo tujhe mitane,
Ve shastra honge be-asar
Tera rachne wala tujh par,
Rakhta hai apni nazar. Gin, gin....
