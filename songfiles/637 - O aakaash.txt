Song 637 - O aakaash

O aakaash aur surya chandrama,
Stuti karo sara bhumandal.
Verse 1
Yeeshu ne tujh se prem kiya,
Tere liye apna praan diya;
Narak se aatma ko bacha liya,
Anant jeevan sab ko diya.
Verse 2
Anugrahkari priya Yeeshu,
Dharmi hokar woh dukh saha;
Hara diya bairi shaitan ko,
Mrityu par Yeeshu vijayee hua.
Verse 3
Dooton ne sunaya susamachar,
Yeeshu ji uthha hai zarur;
Jaake batao yeh samachar,
Baaje bajaate gaate jai-jai-kaar.
