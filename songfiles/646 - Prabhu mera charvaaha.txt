Song 646 - Prabhu mera charvaaha

Prabhu mera charvaaha,
Prabhu mera rakhwaala
Mujhe kuch ghati nahi hai (2)
Sukhdaayee jharne ke paas,
Aguvai karta hai meri;
Dharm ke maarg mein woh,
Sarvada le jaata hai. (2)
Shatruon ki upasthiti me,
Aatidhya karta hai mera;
Sir par tel se mujhe,
Abhishek karta hai mera. (2)
Ghor andhakaar se bhari,
Tharai mein ho kar chalun;
Koi haani se na darungaa,
Kyon ki Prabhu saath hai mera
Bhalai aur karuna jeevanbhar,
Nischay banee rahegi,
Prabhu ke mandir mein,
Sarvadaa main vaas karunga (2)
