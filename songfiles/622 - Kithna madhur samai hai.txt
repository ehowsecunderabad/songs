Song 622 - Kithna madhur samai hai

Kithna madhur samai hai (2)
Jab mera Yeshu mujhko
Charano me raha bithatha
Mujh ko khali karke
Athma se bhartha jatha.
Verse 1
Mujhko hari hari ghas charatha
Jeevan jal bhi pilatha (2)
Mujko hai woh bachatha
Jeevan sukh ko latha 'Kithna'
Verse 2
Chahe mai ghor andhkar me chalun
Hani se bhi na darun
Therey sothe aur lathi sey
Man mera shanti hai patha 'Kithna'
