Song 633 - Naare lagao re gaao re

Naare lagao re gaao re,
Jhanda Masih ka phairao re

Halleluiah (4)

Verse 1
Jeevan se uljha hua rahi,
Yeeshu ke paas tum aao re
Gum ki ye raahen sab chhoro,
Zindagi mein ab kuchh joro re.

Verse 2
Lamba safar zindagi ka,
Sachchaai koi na jane re.
Aankhen upar ko lagaao,
Yeeshu ke paas tum aao re.

Verse 3
Yeeshu kurbaan hua hai,
Paapon ka ant hua hai.
Khushiaan woh baat raha hai,
Prem ki dhara wohi hai.
