Song 653 - Tere pe baddate kadam

Chorus 1
Tere dar pe baddate kadam,
Tere mandir mein aa jaayen hum,
Tere darshan ke aatur hain hum,
Stuti karte Prabhu hardam.

Verse 1
Chham-chhamati ye ghunghru ki
dhun,
Varnan karte tera har sugun,
Naache gaate uthaate kadam,
Paane hum sab tera darshan.

Verse 2
Mangal paavanmaya mandir mein hum
Laate hum sab ke jeevan ke gum,
Paate tum mein woh shaanti param,
Yaachna karte hum hardam.

Verse 3
Kaisa sundar madhur ye jeevan,
Tum se paaya hamne vardaan,
Khushion se gaata ye mera man,
Dhanya, dhanya mere bhagwan.
