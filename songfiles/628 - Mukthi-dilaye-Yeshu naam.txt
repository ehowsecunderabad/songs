Song 628 - Mukthi-dilaye-Yeshu naam

Mukthi-dilaye-Yeshu naam '
Shanthi dilaye Yeshu naam

Verse 1
Charani me thoone janam liya Yeshu
Kroos par huva kurbaan

Verse 2
Yeshu hai dayaa ka bahetha sagar
Yeshu hai daatha mahaan

Verse 3
Kroos par apna khoon bahaya
Sara chukaaya daam

Verse 4
Hum sub ke paapon ko mitane
Yesu huwa balidaan

Verse 5
Hum par Yeshu krupakarna
Hum hain paapi naadaan.
