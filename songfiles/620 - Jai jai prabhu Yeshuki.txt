Song 620 - Jai jai prabhu Yeshuki

Jai jai prabhu Yeshuki (2),
Hum ko bachane aaya jagath me
Uski stuti karo ' ah,ha,ha,ha,ha,ha,
oh,ho,ho,ho,ho, ho,
La,la,la,la,la,la, Halleluya Amen.
Verse 1
Khoon ki dhara bahati suli se,
Usme dhule sab paap,
Dholo ab tum apne hriday ko,
Usme rahe na daag 'Jai, Jai'
Verse 2
Grahan karo tum aaj Yeshu ko,
Prem ki bahati dhar
Usko banalo khewanhara,
naav lagado paar 'Jai Jai'
Verse 3
Vapas aata mera Prabhuji,
Le jayega saath
Aasha meri ab tho yahi hai,
Tum bhi chalo mere saath 'Jai Jai'
