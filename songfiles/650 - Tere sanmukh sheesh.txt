Song 650 - Tere sanmukh sheesh

Chorus 1
Tere sanmukh sheesh navaate,
He jag ke kartaar;
Doobe huon ko de do sahaara,
Kar do bera paar

Verse 1
Paap ke baadal sar par chhaye
Ghira hua toofaan,
Tum bin nayaa kaun sambhale
Mere Prabhu mahaan,
Aake bachaa lo praan hamare,
Jag ke khevanhaar

Verse 2
Janam ke andhon ko di aankhen
Rogi liye bachaaye,
Paap kshama kiye sab paapin ke,
Murde diye jilaaye,
Paapi hriday humbhi laaye,
Dho do paalanhaar.

Verse 3
Sundar pakshi, parwat, saagar
Sabke srijan haar,
Aake viraajo man mandir mein,
Bande karen pukaar,
Vyakul hriday tujhh ko pukaare,
Aa ja taaranhaar.
