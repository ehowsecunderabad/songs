Song 659 - Aao hum Yahova ka

Aao hum Yahova ka
dhanyawad karen,
Apne saare hriday se
vandana karen,
Uske phatakon mein stuti karen,
Aur lal-ka-ren

Verse 1
Usne banaya hamen,
Woh hai hamara adhaar,
Usne di hamko saans,
Woh hai hamara udhaar,
Uski ho jai-jai, ho aradhana
Woh hai sabhon ka Pradhan

Verse 2
Apne saare tan, man se ham,
Prabhu ki stuti karen,
Woh hai shifa aur najaat,
Uski prashansa karen,
Woh hai jag ka trata aur taranhaar
Uski ho-jai-jai sada
