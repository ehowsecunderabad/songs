Song 624 - Masiha bina, hamara

Masiha bina, hamara,
koyi nahin apna sahara
Ye hai Prabhu mera, Ye hai Prabhu tera
Ye hai mera, Ye hai tera, Masiha, hamara.

Verse 1
Tere charan, tere sharan
Paawan kare hamko, dukh ke mare,
Sab hain bechare, aye tere dware
Ye hai mera, ye hai tera,
Sab ka paalan hara 'Masiha'

Verse 2
Thu hai tara, Yishu pyara
Sab ka paalan hara, Dukh ke mare,
Sab hain bechare, Aye tere dware
Ye hai mera, Ye hai tera, sab ka
paalan hara. 'Masiha'
