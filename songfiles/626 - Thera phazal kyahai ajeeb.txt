Song 626 - Thera phazal kyahai ajeeb

Thera phazal kya hai ajeeb
Mere jeevan me bada
Ab tak sambhala mujhey
Aur bhi chahiye thera phazal mujhey.

Verse 1
Bach gaya khathre se main
Yeh bhi hai thera phazal
Sarey pap mere mit gaye hain
Avinashi jeeven paaliya hai
Usey sambhalne ka phazal deydey mujhe
'Thera phazal'

Verse 2
Haleem jo hai uspar 
rahtha hai thera phazal
Is jeevan ke liye kafi hai
Thakath aur thandrusthi ke saath
Seva karneka phazal deydey mujhe
'Thera phazal'

Verse 3
Prabhu aaneke din mein milega
bada phazal
Jagthe huye paane ke liye
Yishu se mai milne ke liye
Daya ke hath ka phazal deydey mujhe
'Thera phazal'
