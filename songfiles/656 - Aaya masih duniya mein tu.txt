Song 656 - Aaya masih duniya mein tu

Chorus 1
Aaya masih duniya mein tu,
Paapion ko, bachaane ko ;
Laaye imaan, jo bete par,

Verse 1
Duniya gunahon mein, doob rahi thi,
Saadik gumrah, ho rahe the ;
Chhora aasmaan, bana insaan,
Mili najaat, is duniya ko.

Verse 2

paida hua, hai Krisht nidaan.

Verse 3
Aalimon ne, kitabon mein
Pari paidaish, ki tafsil ;

Verse 4
