Song 638 - Mil kar ham sanna

Mil kar ham sanna teri gaate,
Sare jahan ko sunate,
Jai jai ho teri Prabhu,
Mahima ho teri Prabhu,
Sab ko ham yah git sunate
Verse 1
Saare jahan ka sahara,
Dube huon ka (kinara) - 2,
Naiya hamari dole,
Maunjho mein khaaye (hilore) - 2,
Lechal kinare lejaa,
Ban kar tu maanjhi lejaa,
Ham sab ko paar lagaade.
Verse 2
Chaaron taraf hain ghataayen,
Ghanghor baadal
(hain chhayen) - 2;
Sujhe nahin ab kinara,
Tu hi hamara (sahara) - 2.
Lechal kinare lejaa
Ban kar tu maanjhi lejaa,
Ham sab ko paar lagaade.
Verse 3
Paapon mein jeevan hamara,
Kashton ko jhel raha hai;
Kaise bachenge saare,
Paapi yeh jeevan hamare.
To hi hamari aashaa,
Tu hi hamara raja,
Ham sab ko paar lagaade.
