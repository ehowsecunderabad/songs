Song 635 - Shoonya se le ke tune

Shoonya se le ke tune mujhe,
Rach liya apne he roop mein;
Prem kiya hai anant prem se,
Diya putra meri mukti ke liye.

Anokha pyar hai tera,
Karunga stuti teri main sarvada (2)

Verse 2
Jag mein aayaa Yeeshu swarg
chhorke,
Mera saara dand sah liya usne;
Kore khakhe krus uthake Yeeshu ne,
Mujhe mukti aur changai de di hai.

Anokha pyar hai tera,
Karunga stuti teri main sarvada (2)

Verse 3
Avinashi anant mera,
Dhanya sangati Yeeshu ke saath;
Swarg mein dhanya teri santan,
Payenge Yeeshu se jalali pyar.

Anokha pyar hai tera,
Karunga stuti teri main sarvada (2)

Verse 4
Ann, vastra aur sabhi aashishen,
Di mujhe tune bharpuri se;
Khatron aur sab musibaton mein,
Aankh ki putli jaise sambhala
mujhe.

Anokha pyar hai tera,
Karunga stuti teri main sarvada (2)
